Upcoming Events

Stay up-to-date with the latest events in Peterborough

  • May 24th: Peterborough Arts Festival - Celebrating local artists and performers
  • June 8th: Summer Music Concert in the Park - Enjoy live music under the stars
  • June 18th-20th: Peterborough Food and Drink Festival - A culinary extravaganza
  • July 1st: Independence Day Fireworks Spectacular - A dazzling display of fireworks
  • August 14th-15th: Peterborough Garden Show - Explore stunning garden displays

Places to Eat and Drink

Discover the best dining options in Peterborough

  • The Riverside Restaurant - A waterfront dining experience with a diverse menu
  • La Trattoria - Authentic Italian cuisine in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere
  • The Greenhouse Cafe - A charming cafe offering organic and locally sourced dishes
  • Fire & Spice - A fusion restaurant combining flavors from around the world
  • The Pint & Platter Pub - A traditional English pub serving classic pub fare and a wide selection of beers
