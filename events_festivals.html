Football Events in Peterborough

Peterborough is a city known for its vibrant sports culture, and football is no exception. The city hosts various football
events throughout the year, offering exciting matches and opportunities for football enthusiasts to
cheer on their favorite teams. Some notable football events in Peterborough include:

  • Peterborough United FC Matches

    Date: Check the schedule

    Peterborough United FC, also known as "The Posh," is the local professional football team. Attending their matches at the Weston Homes Stadium is a fantastic way to experience the thrill of live football.

  • Peterborough Sunday League

    Date: Ongoing

    The Peterborough Sunday League showcases local football talent as amateur teams compete in various divisions. The league provides an exciting platform for players and fans alike to engage in grassroots football.

  • Peterborough Junior Alliance League

    Date: Ongoing

    The Peterborough Junior Alliance League focuses on youth football, providing opportunities for young players to develop their skills and compete against other teams in the area.
